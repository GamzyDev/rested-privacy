Rested Privacy Policy

Publisher: Gamzy
Effective date: June 10, 2026
Last updated: June 10, 2026

Rested is a Windows desktop application that processes local data on the user's device to display local Codex usage and productivity information. Rested does not sell personal data, does not use personal data for advertising, and is not designed to transmit local Codex usage, session, thread, activity, or settings data to Gamzy servers or to third-party advertising or data broker services.

Scope of this policy

This Privacy Policy explains what information Rested may access, collect, store, or transmit; how that information is used; how it is stored and protected; the types of parties to whom information may be disclosed; and the controls users have over their information.

Rested is an independent application published by Gamzy. Rested is not affiliated with, endorsed by, sponsored by, or operated by OpenAI or Microsoft.

Information Rested may access

Rested is a full-trust Windows desktop application. To provide its functionality, Rested may access and process local files and settings on the user's Windows device, including local Codex usage, session, thread, activity, configuration, and related application files selected by the user or available in the user's local app data locations.

Depending on the user's local files, this information may include usage statistics, session and thread metadata, timestamps, project names, project paths, file paths, command or activity metadata, limit information, locally stored settings, and text contained in local Codex-related files. Some of this information may be personal information if it identifies, relates to, or can be associated with a user or another person.

How Rested uses information

Rested uses the information it accesses only to provide local app functionality, including displaying usage statistics, session and thread summaries, activity information, local limits, status information, and related productivity information inside the app.

Rested does not use personal information for advertising, interest-based advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

Local storage and retention

Rested stores its own settings and any local cached app data on the user's Windows device. Data shown by Rested is derived from local files on the user's device. Rested does not operate a Gamzy cloud account system for local Codex data.

Local app settings and cached data remain on the device until removed by the user, replaced by normal app operation, cleared through Windows app storage controls where available, manually removed where permitted by Windows, or removed when the app is uninstalled.

Transmission, sharing, and disclosure

Rested is not designed to transmit local Codex usage data, session data, thread data, activity data, local app data, or local settings to Gamzy servers, advertisers, data brokers, or other third parties.

If the user chooses to contact Gamzy for support by email, the user may provide personal information such as an email address, message content, screenshots, logs, or diagnostic details. Gamzy uses support communications only to respond to support, privacy, or product questions.

If Microsoft Store, Windows, or related Microsoft services are used for purchase, trial, licensing, installation, updates, analytics, crash reporting, certification, security, or distribution, Microsoft may process information according to Microsoft's own privacy terms and platform policies.

Gamzy may disclose information if required to comply with applicable law, legal process, or enforceable governmental request, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of users, Gamzy, Microsoft Store services, or the public.

Security

Rested is designed to keep local Codex data local to the user's Windows device and to avoid unnecessary transmission of that data. Local files, local settings, and local cached data are protected by the user's Windows account, device security, storage permissions, backup configuration, and operating system protections.

Users should protect their Windows account, device, local files, and backups, because anyone with access to the user's Windows account or local storage may be able to access local files and app data stored on that device.

User controls and access

Users control the local files and folders available on their device. Users can stop Rested from displaying information from local Codex files by deleting, moving, or restricting access to those files. If the user deletes, moves, or restricts access to files that Rested reads, Rested may no longer be able to display information from those files.

Users can review their local Codex files and Rested's locally displayed information on their own device. Users can remove Rested's local settings and cached app data by clearing the app's local data through Windows settings where available, removing local app data manually where permitted by Windows, or uninstalling Rested.

Sensitive information and children

Rested is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect children's personal information. Rested is not designed to collect health data, financial data, contacts, photos, microphone recordings, camera recordings, or precise location data.

Because Rested reads local files selected by the user or available on the user's device, users should avoid storing sensitive personal information in local files that they do not want displayed or processed by Rested.

Changes to this policy

Gamzy may update this Privacy Policy when Rested's functionality, data practices, legal requirements, or Microsoft Store requirements change. The updated policy will be posted on this page with a revised last updated date.
